# Proof of Presence — LUKSO Badge Generator

A mini app for the LUKSO blockchain that lets you connect your Universal Profile and download a personalized "connected offchain" badge.

## What it does
- Connect your Universal Profile (UP)
- Generate a personal badge with your profile photo
- Choose from 7 badge styles
- Download as PNG to use with the Proof of Presence app

## How to use
1. Open the app
2. Connect your Universal Profile via the UP Browser Extension (desktop) or UP mobile app (mobile)
3. Choose a badge style
4. Download your badge
5. Open the Proof of Presence app in your UP mobile app to generate a QR code
6. Let others scan your QR to mint your badge as an NFT in their Universal Profile

## Built on
- LUKSO blockchain
- LSP3 Universal Profiles
- LSP8 NFT standard
